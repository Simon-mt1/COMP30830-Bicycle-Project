FAQ's
How can I see when the stations are busy?
On the map, you will see icons at every station. There are two buttons at the top of the page: "Available Bikes" and "Available Stations". Upon clicking these, the number in each station icon will display accordingly. You can also click into a station to see more detailed info.
I want to plan a trip to Dublin. When is the best time to rent a bike?
When you click on any station icon, trending data will display at the bottom of the sidebar. This shows how busy that station is during the day, helping you plan your trip.
I heard Dublin is a rainy city? How will I know if it will be raining?
There's a weather icon at the top of the page showing the current temperature and conditions. Clicking this icon brings up a 48-hour forecast so you can prepare ahead.
I don't know my way around Dublin. How will I know where to find the stations?
This app has built-in directions! After clicking a station, click the “Directions” button to get directions from your current location.
There are so many stations in the city! How do I know which one is closest to me?
At the top of the page is a “Closest Station” button. Click it to highlight the nearest station and show it in the sidebar.
